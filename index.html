Program Overview

Do you really know what your customers want?
Carnegie Mellon University, a pioneer in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI),
is offering a 3-day workshop that will make you rethink the way you think about customers and what they want.

What Is It?

CMU has spent the last 20 years collaborating with the luminaries changing our world like Google, NASA, Bloomberg, and Bank of America. The CMU@NYC Executive Boot Camp is your opportunity to reimagine the foundation of your business and see the problems you didn’t know exist.

To compete with the rapid innovation and technologies in today’s world, you must be prepared to bring your customers more than a product or service; you have to create an experience that people love. We can teach you how to do that.

How It Works


  • Day 1

    To design a delightful user experience, you have to first deeply understand your customers’ emotions and desires. Day 1 is all about relearning experience.

  • Day 2

    Design Thinking is a different way to look at facets of a business problem. Day 2 is about relearning problems and how you approach them.

  • Day 3

    The homestretch. Day 3 is when you will apply the experiences and problems from your business and begin designing solutions, failing, learning, and repeating while journey mapping, producing field research, and building prototypes to begin delivering the human-centered experiences your customers will love.

Who's It For?

Design Thinking is not design as you know it. You don’t need a design background. You don’t need to have heard of it before today. You do need a desire to push your business ahead and to deliver what your customer doesn’t know they want.


This executive boot camp is for:

  • Senior-Level Business Executives
  • Decision Makers and Movers
  • Forward Thinking Leaders
  • Change Motivators and Revolutionaries
